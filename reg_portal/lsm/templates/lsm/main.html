LSM 2023

Local Startups' Meet

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur has successfully influenced students who are enthusiastic about the startup ecosystem since 2006. With our aim to influence people who have already ventured into creating their own enterprise we proudly launch the 5th chapter of the flagship initiative Local Startups’ Meet (LSM) 2022. LSM 2022 will be held around the first fortnight of October in 12 Cities. Local Startups’ Meet is an Invite-only, well-curated event, chiefly embodying eminent Investors, venture capitalists, and Influencers along with the early-stage startups, and some incubated startups that are currently working on their Alpha or Beta stage of product development. The event is designed to provide startups in Tier 1 cities with Networking and pitching opportunities.
